ACME Corporation - Vacation and Time Off Policy

1. Annual Leave Entitlement
   - Full-time employees: 20 days per year
   - Part-time employees: Pro-rated based on hours worked
   - New employees: No vacation time in first 3 months

2. Requesting Time Off
   - Submit requests at least 2 weeks in advance
   - Manager approval required
   - Maximum 2 consecutive weeks at a time

3. Public Holidays
   - Company observes 10 public holidays per year
   - List published at start of each year

4. Sick Leave
   - Up to 10 days per year
   - Doctor's note required for absences over 3 days
   - Unused sick days do not carry over

5. Unused Vacation
   - Up to 5 days can be carried over to next year
   - Remaining days are forfeited
   - No cash payout for unused vacation

This policy is effective January 1, 2025.
